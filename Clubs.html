Newtonhill Netball Club

We are affiliated to Netball Scotland and Aberdeen & District Netball Association. We have 3 adult teams in the District League and U15 teams in the Junior League. The coaches hold UKCC qualifications. Players are given the opportunity to pursue coaching and umpire qualifications as well as playing. Newtonhill is a friendly club and we welcome players of all ages and abilities.
